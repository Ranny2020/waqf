Proposed Tikau Zakat and Waqf Plaza Project

  • Project Description: Tikau Emirate Waqf Plaza
  • Location of the Project: Potiskum, Yobe state, Nigeria
  • Estimated Cost: ₦ 155,000,000.000

The Tikau Zakat and Waqf Plaza is envisioned as a central hub for charitable activities and community engagement. This project aims to create a vibrant space where individuals can contribute to social welfare through Zakat and Waqf initiatives.

Key features of the plaza include:

  • A dedicated area for Zakat collection and distribution.
  • Community facilities for educational workshops and seminars.
  • Spaces for local businesses to promote ethical products and services.
  • Green areas for relaxation and community events.

This plaza will serve as a model for community-driven philanthropy, fostering a spirit of giving and collaboration among residents.

Programs

This program is schedule to take place at the Government Lodge, Sabon Garin.
