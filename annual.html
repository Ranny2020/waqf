The Journey So Far

The activities of Tikau Zakat and Waqf committee from 2002 till date has been inspiring and rewarding. The committee introduced many strategies on which Waqf funds are utilized. From the preceding table of yearly zakat and Waqf collections the sum of ₦ 44,124,814 was collected and distributed to the needy.

Current Challenges

Establishment of Jumma'st Masjids: Jumma'at Masjids have sprung up across the nooks and crannies of the emirate. As at 2022 there are 40 jumma'at Masjids in Tikau Emirate. Construction, expansions, renovations, salaries and allowances of Imams and Na'ibs, provisions of regalia and accessories for Imams, provision of Holy Qur'an, Islamic books and Journals, Workshops/retreats for Imams are being organized annually (specially before the mouth of Ramadan) are some of the current challenges of the committee.

Establishment and Management of Islamiyya Schools

Before the establishment of Tikau Zakat and Waqf committee many villages in Tikau Emirate have no access to formal islamic education. In 2002, the committee started with the establishment of 10 Islamiyya schools. It is gratifying to note the impressive increase in the number of Islamiyya schools in the emirate from 10 in 2002 to 74 in 2022. With regards to enrolment of students, married women, and teachers.
These successes can be attributed to the firm commitment of his royal highness Mai of Tikau with support of the zakat and Waqf committee in propagating Islamic education throughout the emirate.

It is however worthy to note that yearly increase in the number of Islamic schools, students/married women enrolment, teachers, school furniture (in villages where there are no primarys class rooms), other instructional materials as well as allowances for islamiyya tutors goes with it increasing financial burden on the Zakat and Waqf funds.

Routine Da'awah Activitics

Over the years, the Zakat and Waqf committee has been collaborating with FOMWAN, Yobe state chapter, in conducting series of Da'awah activities in designated centres across the emirate. Lectures, practical demonstration, Questions and answers sessions on selected topics were delivered. Notably, in the year 2022, the committee in collaboration with islamic teachers from yobe state FOMWAN organization conducted a workshop with practical demostration titled "Jinya da wankan gawa a musulunci", which recieved the coverage of NTA and Sunnah Tv. The committee further introduced other routine Da'awah prayers, Usually inform of weekly pre-khudduba in all Jumma'at Mosques within the Tikau.

Distribution of Hijab at Subsidized Rate

In an effort to encourage Islamic values and ensure modest Islamic dress code in Tikau, the Tikau Zakat and Waqf committee initiated Hijab distribution programmee with 50% discount. Initially, the programme targeted students of Islamiyya schools, but later almost all the women of the Tikau Emirate accepted Hijab as their mode of dressing, contrary to how they were before the introduction of the hijab distribution programme.

With gratitude to Allah (S.W.A), it is worth noting that Tikau Emirate remains the only Emirate in the state that has an established functional Tikau Zakat and Waqf committee. This has attracted the attention of the state government which issue a letter of commendation to the Emirate in recognition of the tremendous efforts.
