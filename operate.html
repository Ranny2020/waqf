HOW WE OPERATE

At Tikau Waqf Foundation, we prioritize robust operations and governance to guide our impactful investments and grants. We are committed to integrity and transparency, and our policies clearly spell out our operational rules.

Policies

Better Governance, Better World.

Investment Policy

This policy defines our investment objectives, and help us:

  1. Manage and mitigate risk prudently
  2. Carry out investment with consistency, efficiency, transparency and accountability
  3. Achieve compliance with regulatory requirements

Governance Framework

The purpose of our governance framework is to set out the roles, responsibilities, and procedures for the effective and efficient conduct of its business, ensuring that the charity can successfully deliver its strategic aims, objectives, and priorities.
